About Us

Founded on hope, driven by community.

Our Mission

Recovery Together was built to create a safe, supportive, and judgment-free space for people in recovery. We believe in friendship, community, and the power of human connection. Whether you’re on day one or year ten, you belong here.

Who We Are

We are people with families, with stories, and with a passion for helping others. Our team is committed to quality, integrity, and building a space that serves the community with care.
